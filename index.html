The shima shima collection

Our Shima Shima collection, taken from the Japanese word for stripes, is inspired by the fabric designs of late 19th century Japan. Shima stripes are often used to convey happiness, playfulness and good feeling- we hope they spark those feelings in you! Available as art prints and printed on clothing.
